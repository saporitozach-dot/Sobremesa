Brand Guidelines  ·  v1.0

The idea

Sobremesa (Spanish) is the time spent lingering at the table after a meal — talking, not scrolling. The logo holds the whole brand in one image: a speech bubble (conversation, presence) wrapped around a crossed fork and knife (the meal). Talk over food. Phones away.

The app ships with the platform system font (San Francisco on iOS, Roboto on Android) so it feels native. The wordmark uses Helvetica Neue; convert it to outlines in any exported logo so the brand never depends on an installed font.

Voice & taglines

Phone-Free Dining Be here for the meal. Linger at the table. Presence over screens. The conversation is the main course.

Tone: warm, hospitable, never preachy. We invite, we don't scold. We celebrate presence rather than shame the phone.

Usage

Do

  • Lead with Forest green; let Amber be the one thing that glows.
  • Keep generous clear space around the mark.
  • Use the icon-only mark for small spaces and avatars.
  • Set body text in Cream or Forest for contrast.

Don't

  • Don't recolor the logo outside the palette (no blue — we're the antidote to blue apps).
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or add drop shadows to the mark.
  • Don't set long text in Amber on Forest — it fails legibility.
  • Don't place the mark on a busy photo without a solid backing.
